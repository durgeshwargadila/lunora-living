Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity, typically ranging from 4 to 12 weeks.

What is your design process?

We start with a consultation, follow with concept development, create detailed plans, and finalize implementation with your approval at each stage.

Do you work within specific budgets?

Yes, we specialize in creating beautiful designs at every budget level. We discuss your budget upfront to ensure realistic expectations.

Can you design for renovation projects?

Absolutely! We handle both new construction and renovation projects, working with contractors and construction teams.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual design consultations for clients who prefer remote meetings. We can work with you wherever you are.
