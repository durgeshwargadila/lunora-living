About Lunora Spaces

Creating elegant spaces that inspire and transform lives

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Lunora Spaces emerged from a simple vision: to create interior spaces that are not just beautiful, but truly transformative. Our name, inspired by the word "lunar," reflects our belief in bringing light and elegance into every space we touch.

What started as a small design studio has grown into a full-service interior design firm recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. Today, we're proud to have completed over 150 projects and served more than 500 satisfied clients.

Our Mission

At Lunora Spaces, our mission is to create exceptional interior environments that enhance the way people live. We believe that thoughtful design has the power to improve daily experiences, boost well-being, and create lasting memories. We're dedicated to bringing our clients' visions to life while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

Our Values

  • Creativity: We push boundaries and explore new design possibilities
  • Integrity: We maintain honesty and transparency in all our dealings
  • Excellence: We never compromise on quality or attention to detail
  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly materials and practices
  • Collaboration: We work closely with clients to bring their dreams to reality

Our Journey

Key milestones in Lunora Spaces' growth and success

2014 - The Beginning

Lunora Spaces founded with a small team of passionate designers and a big vision for transforming homes.

2016 - Recognition

Our innovative designs began winning awards and recognition from industry publications and design councils.

2018 - Expansion

Expanding our team to 15 designers and establishing our first flagship showroom in the city center.

2020 - Digital Innovation

Launched our virtual design consultation services, allowing us to serve clients globally during challenging times.

2022 - Sustainability Focus

Became certified as an eco-friendly design firm, pioneering sustainable interior design solutions.

2024 - Today

Proud to have completed 150+ projects and serving over 500 clients while maintaining our commitment to excellence.
